What to expect

A thorough, relaxed eye test

Your optometrist will check how clearly you see, measure your prescription, and examine the health of your eyes — then take the time to talk you through the results and your options.

  • Bring your current glasses or contact lenses and your medical-aid card.
  • Most eye tests take around 30 minutes.
  • Choose your new eyewear on the day, from our in-store range.
