Our Story

Rooted in the
Eastern Cape

Born from a love of the ocean and a passion for fresh, honest food, The Reef Cafe has been serving the East London community since 2018. Our kitchen celebrates the bountiful waters of the Wild Coast and the rich agricultural heritage of our region.

Every dish tells a story of local fishermen, farmers, and foragers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. From line-caught fish to organic produce, we believe the best ingredients need little intervention.
