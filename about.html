About Manchester Medical Centre

Three trusted practices, brought together to make quality healthcare simpler and more convenient for families in Pietermaritzburg's Northern Suburbs.

Our story

One centre, three practices, under one roof

Manchester Medical Centre is a new multidisciplinary centre at Manchester Mall in Rosedale, Pietermaritzburg. We bring together three established practices — New Sight Optometry, The Smile Clinic Dentistry and Ms N Paruk's physiotherapy practice — so your family can look after your eyes, teeth and physical health in one convenient place.

Each practice is run by its own registered, experienced practitioner, but together we share a warm reception, easy online booking and a genuine commitment to caring, unhurried treatment. Whether you're here for an eye test, a dental check-up or rehabilitation after an injury, you'll be looked after by people who take the time to listen.

Our approach

What you can expect from us

Patient-centred care

We listen first, explain clearly, and involve you in every decision about your treatment — no rushing, no jargon.

Family-focused

Care for every stage of life, from children's first eye tests and dental visits to rehabilitation and preventative screening.

Registered & professional

All our practitioners are registered with the Health Professions Council of South Africa (HPCSA) and practise to its ethical standards.

The practicalities

Easy to reach, easy to park

Being inside Manchester Mall means everything is convenient — do your shopping, see the optometrist, dentist or physiotherapist, and be on your way.

  • Hundreds of free, enclosed and monitored parking bays
  • Ground-floor, wheelchair-friendly access
  • Central location in Pietermaritzburg's Northern Suburbs
  • Medical aids welcome — claims submitted directly
  • Appointments online or by phone — walk-ins welcome
Your privacy

Your information is protected

We take the confidentiality of your health information seriously and handle your personal information in line with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). New patients are asked to complete a short registration and consent form on their first visit.
